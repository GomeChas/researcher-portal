About us:

Here at MyT Codon, we are passionately invested in unlocking mysteries of Mitochondrial Disease. Our sole focus is to combine modern DNA sequencing with state-of-the-art gene therapies and AI to eradicate all symptoms of these disorders. Our approach leverages our vast data of mitochondrial disease phenotypes, the 37 mitochondrial genes and regulatory RNAs that make up the 16569 base pairs of the mitochondrial genome, and the ever-growing list of novel gene therapies of which there are greater than 1000 to date. Harnessing AI, we then offer our patients and providers precise, tailored treatment options.

To satisfy regulatory requirements, our Research Portal allows our staff to catalog the validation work completed in creating the chimeraplastic therapeutic agents used in both clinical trials and FDA approved treatments. Regulations dictate that each validated therapy needs to have an appropriate sign-off from a credentialed staff member, and a validation date for when all sensitivity, specificity, precision, and accuracy measurements satisfy ISO guidelines.
