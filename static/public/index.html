To satisfy regulatory requirements, our Research Portal allows our staff to catalog the validation work completed in creating the chimeraplastic therapeutic agents used in both clinical trials and FDA approved treatments. Regulations dictate that each validated therapy needs to have an appropriate sign-off from a credentialed staff member, and a validation date for when all sensitivity, specificity, precision, and accuracy measurements satisfy ISO guidelines.
